# Privacy Policy - POE-EDU Platform

**Last Updated: June 2024**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the POE-EDU platform ("Platform") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your data.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Authentication Credentials**: Poe.com session tokens and API keys (stored locally)
- **Messages and Conversations**: Text messages sent through the platform
- **Account Information**: Email, username (if applicable)
- **Technical Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data**: Features used, frequency of use, session duration
- **Device Information**: Device type, browser, operating system
- **Log Data**: Server logs including timestamps, errors, and requests
- **Cookies**: Session cookies for authentication (if applicable)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

- **Service Provision**: Delivering POE-EDU functionality
- **Improvement**: Analyzing usage to improve features
- **Security**: Detecting and preventing abuse or unauthorized access
- **Legal Compliance**: Fulfilling legal obligations
- **Communications**: Notifying you of updates or security issues
- **Troubleshooting**: Diagnosing technical problems

## 4. Data Protection and Security

### 4.1 Security Measures

We implement:
- HTTPS encryption for data in transit
- Secure storage practices for sensitive data
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and user authentication
- Non-root user execution in containers

### 4.2 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:
- Keeping your authentication tokens private
- Using strong passwords
- Logging out after use
- Reporting security vulnerabilities

## 5. Data Retention

- **Session Data**: Retained during active session only
- **Conversation History**: Retained locally on your device/server
- **Log Files**: Retained for 30 days for security and debugging
- **Account Data**: Retained until account deletion

## 6. Sharing Your Information

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share data with third parties for marketing
- Disclose authentication credentials
- Share conversations without your consent

We MAY share information when:
- Required by law or court order
- Necessary to protect security or prevent harm
- With your explicit consent
- With service providers under confidentiality agreements

### 7.1 Poe.com and Other Providers

When you use POE-EDU, your interactions with third-party services are governed by their privacy policies:

- **Poe.com**: [https://poe.com/privacy](https://poe.com/privacy)

### 7.2 Data Sharing with Third Parties

Your messages are transmitted to third-party AI services as part of normal operation. This means:

- They may retain conversation data

## 8. Cookies and Tracking

- **Session Cookies**: Used for authentication only
- **No Tracking Pixels**: We do not use tracking pixels or analytics cookies
- **Local Storage**: May store settings and preferences locally
- **Third-Party Cookies**: Third-party providers may use their own cookies

### 9.1 Access
- Request access to your personal data
- Export your conversation history

### 9.2 Correction
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Update your account information

### 9.3 Deletion
- Request deletion of your data
- Clear your conversation history

### 9.4 Opt-Out
- Disable data collection features where available
- Opt out of usage analytics

- Right to be informed about data processing

- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")

To exercise these rights, contact us through GitHub issues.

California residents have specific rights under CCPA regarding personal information collection and use.
